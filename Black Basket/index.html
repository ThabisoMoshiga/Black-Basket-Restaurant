Our Story

Since our inception in 1990, Black Basket has been a business with a passion for success and this fact is evident in that today. There are currently five Black Basket restaurants operating across South Africa and one in Botswana.

Drawing on the concept “you are always welcome”, the Black Basket experience provides the opportunity for people from all walks of life to experience quality hearty food, served professionally with a smile.

Black Basket strives to intrigue and delight customers with our unique dishes that only make use of the finest ingredients and the freshest produce.
